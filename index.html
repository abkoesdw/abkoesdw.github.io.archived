Hi

I'm Arief (arabic: عارف, pronounced [ah-r-ee-f]), an Indonesian living in Canada. I'm a husband and a father of two amazing boys. I am a Senior Artificial Intelligence Specialist at General Motors Canada. I received my PhD from the University of Waterloo, Canada, for my dissertation on Large-Scale Time Series Prediction using Deep Learning. During the course of my PhD, I worked on vision-based driver behavior recognition using deep learning as a side project, which has been featured in several news outlets, and in 2016, speech recognition systems at a Waterloo-based start-up company.
Before this, I completed BEng in Engineering Physics from ITB in 2008 and MSc in Control Systems from KFUPM in 2013. For more details, visit this page.
