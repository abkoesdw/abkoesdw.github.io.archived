Hi!

I'm Arief (arabic: عارف, pronounced [ah-r-ee-f]), an Indonesian living in Canada. I'm a husband and a father of two amazing boys. I currently work at General Motors Canada as a Senior Artificial Intelligence Specialist. Before this, I completed my PhD degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Waterloo, Canada, for my dissertation on Large-Scale Time Series Prediction using Deep Learning. Apart from my main research topic, I also worked on vision-based driver behavior recognition and prediction using deep learning, which has been featured on Washington Post, Wired Magazine, Globe and Mail, CBC radio, and Canada's Discovery Channel. Currently, I'm working on Natural Language Understanding (NLU) and time-series systems for automotive applications.
